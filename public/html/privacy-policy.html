Tramona ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

When you register on our platform, make a booking request, or contact us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name

  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed via Stripe)
  • Booking details (destination, dates, budget)

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information that your browser sends whenever you visit our platform. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version

  • Time and date of your visit

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

We may share your information with third parties in the following situations:
